=== GEOMETRIC BLUEPRINT - COORDINATES ===

## Geometric Blueprint

### Part 1: Geometric Context from Question

#### 1. QUESTION OBJECTIVE
Find the distance from vertex A to point M in a cube ABCDEFGH with side length 6 cm, where M is the midpoint of edge FG.

#### 2. GIVEN ELEMENTS
*   **Given Lengths:** Side length of cube = 6 cm.
*   **Given Properties:** 
    *   ABCD is the bottom face.
    *   EFGH is the top face.
    *   E is above A, F is above B, G is above C, H is above D.
    *   M is the midpoint of edge FG.

#### 3. ALL GEOMETRIC ELEMENTS
*   **Points:** A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H, M.
*   **Lines/Segments:** 
    *   Bottom edges: AB, BC, CD, DA.
    *   Top edges: EF, FG, GH, HE.
    *   Vertical edges: AE, BF, CG, DH.
    *   Target segment: AM.
    *   Sub-segments: FM, MG.
*   **Angles:** All face angles (e.g., $\angle DAB, \angle ABC, \angle EAB$) are 90°.
*   **Polygons/Shapes:** 
    *   Faces: ABCD, EFGH, ABFE, BCGF, CDHG, DAEH.
*   **3D Objects:** Cube ABCDEFGH.

### Part 2: Coordinate System and Scale Definition (Calculated)

#### 1. ORIGIN PLACEMENT (CRITICAL)
*   Point **A** is the origin: **(0.000, 0.000, 0.000)**.

#### 2. AXES ALIGNMENT
*   **Primary Axis (X-axis):** Vector $\vec{AB}$ defines the positive X-axis.
*   **Primary Plane (XY-plane):** The bottom face ABCD lies on the XY-plane (Z=0).
*   **Vertical Axis (Z-axis):** Vector $\vec{AE}$ defines the positive Z-axis.

#### 3. SCALE DEFINITION
*   **Method:** Side length of the cube (6 cm) is set to **5.0 units**.
*   **Scale Reference:** 6 cm = 5.0 units, scale factor = 5/6 ≈ 0.8333.
*   All coordinates are calculated using this scale factor.
